Services

Below are the services we can provide for all types of property to meet any needs. Due to all installations being different, some requiring more cable, sensors, etc. we do not have a standard price list. Instead we conduct free site surveys where we can inspect the area and plan how much and what equipment we need to complete the instalation. We will provide a free quote upon our visit, if you are interested in any of the services we provide then please fill out an enquiry form and we will get in touch!

CCTV

One of the best ways to protect your property is to deter unwanted intrusions before they even happen, CCTV cameras are the most successful deterents to achieve this. Having a visual presence of CCTV cameras on your property can drastically reduce the risk of burglaries, criminal damage and anti social behaviour. We offer two types of CCTV systems to suit all budgets, POC (Power Over Coaxial) and IP (Internet Protocol), both systems offer HD quality and remote viewing from anywhere around the world using a smart device, so you can rest knowing you can check on your property at any moment.

Intruder Alarms

Intruder Alarms help to protect your property from burglaries; not only is there a visual presence in the way of a Bell box, which is the first deterent potential burglars will see first, but there is obviously the alarm itself which is connected to a service desk who can contact the police. We have a wide selection of alarm key pads to suit all decors, these are used to activate and deactivate your alarm with fobs. One of the key features of our wireless alarm systems is that if your alarm is set off whilst you are away from your property then you will receive notifications to your phone wherever you are.

Access Control

Our Access control systems cover a wide range of operated locks including; video entry phones, pin code, swipe card and voice activated entry. We can design a system bespoke to you, as access control is dependent on what you want it to do and where. The main benefits of access control is first and foremost to control who can go where and to restrict unauthorised access. This is not only to prevent unauthorised access into your premises but can be used inside also to restric access to rooms/offices or areas containing valuable items.

Fire Alarm Systems

We design bespoke Fire alarm systems for residences and businesses to protect your property and those in it keeping you safe and compliant with all fire legislation. Once designed we can supply and install the system to the correct standards and regulations. If you already have a fire alarm system in place, we can add on additional equipment or replace your existing system all thogether. Part of our aftercare for installs on our fire alarm systems is a compulsory, yearly review to maintain and check all equipment is working correctly.
